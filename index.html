Quiénes somosWho we are

Discípulos que hacen discípulos Disciples who make disciples

Nuestra misión es hacer discípulos que aprendan, vivan y prediquen el evangelio, desarrollando personas comprometidas a impactar su comunidad y sociedad para el avance del Reino de Dios. Our mission is to make disciples who learn, live, and preach the gospel — developing people committed to impacting their community and society for the advancement of God's Kingdom.
